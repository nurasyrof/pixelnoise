Welcome to **Pixel Noise**.

This is a space for thinking out loud about design systems, visual interfaces, and the tools we use to build them.

Good design lives at the intersection of clarity and intention. Every pixel, every interaction, every layout decision carries weight — it either earns the user's trust or quietly erodes it.

## What to expect

Posts here will cover a range of topics:

- **Design systems** — how they're built, why they break, and what makes them last
- **UI/UX craft** — typography, spacing, motion, color theory, and the small decisions that add up
- **Tools and process** — Figma, component libraries, design tokens, and the workflows that connect design to engineering
- **Web technology** — CSS, accessibility, performance, and the browser as a creative medium

## Who this is for

If you're a designer who writes code, an engineer with a taste for aesthetics, or just someone who cares about how digital products feel — this is for you.

Pull up a chair. Let's make some noise.
